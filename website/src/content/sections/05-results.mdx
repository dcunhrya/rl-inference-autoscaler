## Results

All policies are evaluated across **50 episodes**. Higher episode return is better (less negative).

### Benchmark summary

| Policy | Return (mean) | Cost penalty | Latency penalty |
|--------|---------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **PPO** | −692 | 299 | 393 |
| **DQN** | −902 | 263 | 639 |
| Greedy | −17,566 | 98 | 17,468 |
| Target util (HPA) | −9,519 | 171 | 9,348 |
| Fixed (n=4) | −46,821 | 80 | 46,741 |
| Do nothing | −82,099 | 20 | 82,079 |

<PolicyComparisonChart />

PPO achieves the **best mean return** among all policies: roughly **25×** better than greedy and **118×** better than do-nothing on this trace. 
DQN is competitive but trades more latency for lower replica cost.
